Behind Campertje

Who is Campertje?

Campertje is a platform built for and by camper lovers. What started as a student project grew into a passionate initiative helping people discover the freedom of vanlife.

Our focus lies in community, discovery, and practical support â€” to help everyone from beginners to full-time travelers enjoy life on the road.

Why Campertje?

We aim to make camper life accessible and exciting. Whether you're planning your first trip or looking for hidden gems across Europe, Campertje brings the right tips, tools, and stories to your fingertips.

Our mission is to connect, inspire, and empower a growing camper van community with real experiences and reliable guides.
