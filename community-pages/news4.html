3 IKEA Budget Hacks for Camper Van Interiors

Did you know IKEA is a goldmine for camper van organization? With the right items, you can transform a tiny space into a smart, functional, and budget-friendly home on wheels. Here are three of our favorite IKEA finds that are perfect for vanlife — no DIY skills required.

1. SKUBB Storage Boxes

These lightweight, foldable boxes are ideal for keeping your cabinets or under-seat storage tidy. They help you separate clothes, food supplies, or gear — and they fit into most small spaces like they were made for it.

2. KROK Hooks

Simple but effective. These compact hanging hooks are great in small kitchens for utensils, dish towels, or even keys. Mount them inside a cabinet or on a wall — they take up almost no space but add tons of functionality.

3. VARIERA Bin (10L)

Technically a trash bin, but in a camper it’s a multitasking champ. Use it as a compact trash can, laundry basket, or even dry storage. It fits perfectly on the inside of a cabinet door, saving precious floor space.

IKEA proves that camper van living doesn’t have to be expensive or complicated. A few clever pieces can make a big difference in how you live and move in your tiny home on wheels. Functional, flexible, and affordable — exactly what vanlife should be.
