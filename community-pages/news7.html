5 Must-Drive Camper Routes in Europe

Dreaming of a road trip across Europe? There’s nothing quite like exploring the continent from the comfort of your campervan — waking up in the mountains, cooking with a view, and following the road wherever it takes you. Whether you're new to vanlife or a seasoned traveler, these five scenic routes should be at the top of your bucket list.

1. Italy – From Tuscany to the Amalfi Coast

Italy offers a perfect blend of culture, cuisine, and coastlines. Drive through the rolling hills of Tuscany, visit charming villages like San Gimignano, then head south toward the dramatic cliffs and colorful towns of the Amalfi Coast. Every stop is a postcard.

2. Spain – The Northern Coast

Skip the tourist-heavy south and head north. The route from San Sebastián to Santiago de Compostela takes you along rugged coastlines, lush forests, and quiet surf towns. It’s the perfect blend of nature and culture — with plenty of tapas along the way.

3. Scotland – The North Coast 500

Scotland’s answer to Route 66, the NC500 is a breathtaking loop around the northern tip of the country. Think wild beaches, ancient castles, lochs, and some of the most remote roads in the UK. It’s rugged, raw, and unforgettable.

4. Switzerland – Alpine Adventure

If you love mountains, Switzerland is heaven on wheels. From Interlaken to Zermatt, you’ll pass alpine lakes, snowy peaks, and charming mountain villages. Just be prepared for some narrow roads — and don’t forget your camera.

5. Norway – The Fjord Route

Few landscapes are as dramatic as Norway’s fjords. Start in Bergen and work your way through Geirangerfjord, Trollstigen, and the Lofoten Islands. Waterfalls, towering cliffs, and midnight sun await — it's camper heaven.

Whether you want coastal roads or mountain passes, Europe is filled with routes that turn road trips into life-changing adventures. So fuel up, pack light, and let the road guide you.
