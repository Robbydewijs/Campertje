Common Camper Mistakes (and How to Avoid Them)

Last modified: 18 april 2025

Whether you're a seasoned traveler or heading out on your very first camper trip, mistakes are part of the adventure. The good news? You can learn from others and skip the hassle. In this blog, we highlight three classic camper mistakes—and share tips to help you steer clear of them.

Mistake 1: Overpacking

Google Maps is great—until it sends you down a narrow forest road with no room to turn around. Standard navigation tools don’t always take camper size or road restrictions into account. Tip: Use camper-friendly apps like Campercontact or Park4Night. These apps are designed for motorhome travelers, showing suitable routes, safe overnight stops, and practical amenities.

Mistake 3: Parking on an Uneven Surface

A slanted camper isn’t just uncomfortable—it can be a recipe for spilled drinks, bad sleep, and even damage to your fridge. Solution: Use a leveling app or a simple bubble level to check your setup. And always carry leveling blocks to adjust your camper’s position when needed. A flat camper makes a big difference in your comfort.

Final Thoughts

Mistakes are part of the journey—but with a little preparation, you can avoid the most common ones and focus on enjoying the ride. Have you ever made one of these mistakes? Share your experience in the comments or tag your story with #VanlifeLessons.
